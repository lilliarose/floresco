Cycle to School

You’ve all heard of a bike, so what’s stopping you and your classmates from cycling to school? Its not your fault, it is largely due to the lack of safe cycle routes, but with the help of your school, this problem can be fixed.
Most councils now have the ability to create “School Streets”. This is where roads around the school are closed during the start/end to ensure roads are safe for students cycling/walking.
Your school has a lot of data on where their students are travelling from, so if they utilise this, they can give specific requests to local counsellors of where installing cycle paths will make the most difference.
Use this space to talk all things cycling/walking!
