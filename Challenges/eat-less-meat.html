Eat Less Meat

Cows produce methane, and lots of space is used on farming animals, which indirectly leads to deforestation as there is more demand for space.
A great way for your school to reduce its emissions impact is for school canteens/cafes to serve less meat. Post here about your veggie menu successes to inspire other schools to do the same!
